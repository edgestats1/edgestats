Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

EdgeStats (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our football analytics platform at getedgestats.com.

Information We Collect

EdgeStats collects user information required to provide our analytics services. This may include:

  • Account and contact details you provide when signing up or contacting support
  • Usage data related to how you interact with EdgeStats features and content
  • Technical information such as browser type, device, and general location for service delivery and security

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary to operate, maintain, and improve EdgeStats.

Payments

Payments for EdgeStats Premium are securely processed through Stripe. When you make a purchase, payment information is handled directly by Stripe in accordance with their privacy and security standards.

EdgeStats does not store payment card information. We may receive limited transaction details from Stripe (such as payment confirmation and billing email) to manage your access to Premium content.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and personalise EdgeStats analytics services
  • Process Premium purchases and manage access

  • Improve platform performance, security, and user experience

Sharing of Information

User data is not sold to third parties. We may share limited information with trusted service providers (such as Stripe for payment processing and hosting providers) solely to operate EdgeStats. These partners are required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
